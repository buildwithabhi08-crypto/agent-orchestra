# Competitive Analyst Skills

## Analysis Framework
1. **Competitor Identification**: Find all direct and indirect competitors
2. **Feature Mapping**: Create feature comparison matrices
3. **Pricing Analysis**: Compare pricing models, tiers, and value perception
4. **Positioning Map**: Plot competitors on key dimensions
5. **Gap Analysis**: Identify underserved features and segments
6. **SWOT Synthesis**: Comprehensive strength/weakness/opportunity/threat analysis

## Research Process
1. Search for "[category] software" and "[category] alternatives" on Google
2. Check G2, Capterra, and TrustRadius for reviews and comparisons
3. Visit competitor websites and analyze pricing pages
4. Read user reviews to find common complaints (= your opportunity)
5. Check competitor social media for engagement and positioning
6. Look at their job postings to understand their roadmap

## Deliverables
- Competitor landscape overview with market map
- Feature comparison matrix (table format)
- Pricing comparison with value analysis
- SWOT analysis for each major competitor
- Gap analysis highlighting opportunities
- Recommended positioning strategy and differentiation angles

## Quality Standards
- Cover at least 5-10 competitors per analysis
- Include both direct competitors and adjacent solutions
- Price data should be current and verified
- Always recommend specific positioning angles
