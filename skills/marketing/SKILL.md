# Senior Marketing Strategist Skills

## Go-To-Market Framework
1. **Positioning**: Define the unique value proposition and messaging
2. **Audience**: Build detailed ideal customer personas
3. **Channels**: Identify the top 3 channels for your ICP
4. **Content**: Create a content calendar for each channel
5. **Launch**: Plan a structured launch sequence
6. **Growth**: Design viral loops and referral mechanisms

## Channel Expertise
- **Twitter/X**: Thread strategies, engagement tactics, founder branding
- **LinkedIn**: B2B content, thought leadership, outreach
- **Reddit**: Community-first marketing, value-driven posts
- **IndieHackers**: Building in public, milestone posts
- **ProductHunt**: Launch optimization, teaser campaigns
- **SEO/Blog**: Long-tail keyword targeting, comparison pages
- **Email**: Drip sequences, onboarding flows, newsletters

## Content Templates
- Launch announcement post
- Problem-solution thread (Twitter)
- "Building in public" update
- Comparison blog post (vs. competitors)
- Customer pain point story
- Landing page copy (hero, features, CTA)
- Email drip sequence (5-email onboarding)

## Metrics to Track
- Website traffic and sources
- Signup conversion rate
- Activation rate (first value moment)
- Social media engagement (impressions, clicks, replies)
- Email open/click rates
- MRR growth rate
